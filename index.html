Warbler information

Here are some general facts about warblers

  • They are all in the order passeriformes
  • There are over 100 species of warblers found in North America
  • Warblers are migratory species
  • Warblers tend to be small insectivorous species

Warblers can be broken up into two groups, old world and new world.
